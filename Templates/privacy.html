Privacy Policy

Last updated: [June 2026]

This Privacy Policy explains how Duka Store ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

  • Account details you provide when registering: your name, email, and password.
  • Order details: items purchased, order totals, and order history.
  • Payment details processed through M-Pesa. We do not store your M-Pesa PIN or full payment credentials — these are handled directly by Safaricom's M-Pesa system.
  • Any information you send us through our Contact form.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders.
  • To communicate with you about your orders, account, or enquiries.
  • To improve our products and service.

Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in and to remember items in your cart. We do not use cookies for third-party advertising.

Third-Party Services

Payments are processed via Safaricom's M-Pesa platform. We encourage you to review Safaricom's own privacy practices regarding how they handle your payment data.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including storing passwords in hashed (not plain-text) form. However, no method of transmission over the internet is ever completely secure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by reaching out through our Contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Reach us via our Contact page.
